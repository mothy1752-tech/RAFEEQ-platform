About RAFEEQ

Empowering people with physical disabilities in their professional journey.

RAFEEQ is a specialized electronic platform dedicated to supporting and empowering people with physical disabilities to find job opportunities that match their abilities and needs.

Using smart tools based on artificial intelligence, we help our users create professional resumes, recommend the best job matches, and improve the overall employment experience for both individuals and companies.

  • AI-powered Resume Building
  • Accessible Transportation Services
  • Smart Job Matching based on Accessibility
  • Career Guidance and Mentorship
